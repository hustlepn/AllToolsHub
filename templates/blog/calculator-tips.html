5 Essential Tips for Using Our Financial Calculators

Posted on January 18, 2023

Getting Accurate Results

Our calculators help with loans, mortgages, and investments. Follow these tips:

1. Know Your Interest Type

Is your loan simple or compound interest? This dramatically affects calculations.

2. Include All Fees

For accurate loan payments, include processing fees and insurance costs.

3. Consider Inflation

When calculating investment growth, account for 2-3% annual inflation.

4. Use Multiple Scenarios

Try different loan terms to find your optimal payment plan.

5. Save Your Results

Print or screenshot calculations for future reference.
