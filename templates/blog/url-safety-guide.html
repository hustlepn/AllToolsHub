How to Stay Safe When Using Short URLs

Posted on January 21, 2023

Potential Risks

Shortened URLs can hide malicious links. Watch for:

  • Unexpected download prompts
  • Requests for personal information
  • Spelling errors in the destination site
  • Unsecured (non-HTTPS) connections

Safety Features We Provide

  • Automatic malware scanning
  • HTTPS encryption on all links
  • Clear preview of destination domains
  • No tracking of personal data

Best Practices

  1. Only shorten links you trust
  2. Check where the link goes before sharing
  3. Use our URL Shortener for safe links
  4. Report suspicious links to us
