Complete Guide to PDF Tools

Posted on January 17, 2023

What You Can Do

Our PDF tools help you:

  • Merge multiple PDFs into one
  • Split PDFs into separate pages
  • Convert Word to PDF
  • Convert PDF to Word
  • Compress PDF files

Step-by-Step Instructions

To Merge PDFs:

  1. Go to PDF Tools
  2. Select "Merge PDFs"
  3. Upload your files
  4. Arrange the order
  5. Click "Merge" and download

To Convert Word to PDF:

  1. Select "Word to PDF"
  2. Upload your document
  3. Wait for conversion
  4. Download your PDF
