Keeping Your PDFs Secure

Posted on January 22, 2023

Our Security Promise

When you use our PDF tools:

  • Files are processed in memory (not permanently stored)
  • Automatic deletion after 1 hour
  • SSL encrypted transfers
  • No human reviews your documents

How to Protect Sensitive PDFs

  1. Password-protect sensitive files before uploading
  2. Remove metadata (File > Properties in Adobe)
  3. Use our tools on private networks only
  4. Delete downloaded files after use
